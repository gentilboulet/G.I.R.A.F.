Welcome to G.I.R.A.F. new homepage.

This page is presenting a short preview of the unlimiteds possibilities of G.I.R.A.F., or General Intelligent Replicant Assembled for Flooding, a Perl5 IRC Bot relying on POE::IRC and SQLite3. Objectives

G.I.R.A.F. is aimed at everyone wanting a simple framework to develop his own IRC bot, supporting a wide range of modulares add-ons. This modularity is obtained by G.I.R.A.F. by encapsulating a whole lot a irc handling gruntwork and porivding a much simpler API.
